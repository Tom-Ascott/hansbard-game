How to Play HansBard

What is Hansard?

Hansard is the official record of everything said in UK Parliament since 1803. Every speech, debate, and question is transcribed and published, creating a fascinating database of political language.

How to Play

Compare word frequencies across 5 rounds. For each word, guess whether it appears more or less frequently than the previous word in Parliamentary records.

The Theme Twist

Each day features words with a clever double meaning! The connection is revealed when you complete all 5 rounds. Today's theme might surprise you!
